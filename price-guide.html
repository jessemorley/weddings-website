Fine Art Albums

Printed and assembled by Atkins Lab, your traditional Kodak photographic album is hand constructed using Italian book binding machines and techniques.

Their traditional Kodak photographic paper sandwiched with archival adhesives allows stunning double page spreads on single sheets of paper. Each page thick and luxurious ready to be loved and pored over for generations.

All albums are bound with elegant etched linen covers that need to be seen and touched to be believed, available in light or dark options, and include a natural raw linen or black cotton box to store your treasured memories.

Photo selections are a collaborative process; final layout will be undertaken by me.

Album size: 8 x 10.6"
